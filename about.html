Who am I
HELLO

I’m Hassan Muktar, a passionate and creative individual with a strong interest in UI design, graphics, video editing, and branding. I believe that good design is more than just visuals — it’s about telling a story, solving problems, and creating experiences that people can connect with. Currently, I’m a Computer Science student at Northwest University, Kano, where I’m gaining both the technical knowledge and creative foundation to bring digital ideas to life. Studying computer science has helped me better understand how things work behind the scenes, allowing me to design smarter and more user-focused solutions. My journey into the creative world started with a love for visual storytelling. Over time, I taught myself how to design beautiful interfaces, create compelling graphics, and edit videos that capture attention. I also have experience in building brand identities — from logos to full brand systems — helping individuals and businesses stand out in a crowded digital space. I’m always learning and experimenting with new tools, trends, and technologies. Whether it’s wireframing a mobile app, designing a flyer, editing a promo video, or developing a brand strategy, I give every project my full energy and creativity. Beyond design and tech, I’m someone who values teamwork, continuous growth, and meaningful connections. I enjoy collaborating with others, exchanging ideas, and turning vision into reality.
